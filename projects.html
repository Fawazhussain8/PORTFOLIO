ENG-001 · SIEM Engineering
Log Pipeline Optimization & Alert Engineering (Log360)

Context:
SIEM ingesting excessive log volume (~300k/day), resulting in noise and reduced detection efficiency.

What I Did:

  • Designed log filtering strategy to reduce ingestion noise
  • Implemented targeted event collection (Windows Security + Syslog)
  • Excluded low-value and repetitive logs
  • Created alert profiles for critical security events

Key Focus Areas:

  • Privilege escalation monitoring
  • Authentication anomalies (failed logins, off-hours activity)
  • Security log tampering detection

Outcome:

  • Reduced log volume from ~300k → <100k/day
  • Improved signal-to-noise ratio
  • Reduced alert fatigue

Note:
Due to confidentiality, configurations and screenshots are not disclosed. This summary reflects the engineering approach and outcomes.
